About Me

Hi, I'm Harsh Singla, a passionate Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning student at Chandigarh Group of Colleges, Landran. I enjoy building intelligent systems, AI-powered applications, and software solutions that solve real-world problems.

My interests include Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Python Development, and Full-Stack Web Development. I love turning ideas into working products and continuously improving my skills through hands-on projects, hackathons, and self-learning.

Some of my notable projects include Lyra Personal Assistant, an AI-powered desktop assistant, Lyra Chatbot built using Ollama LLMs, a Music Recommendation System, a Weather Forecast System, and Moving Wheel, a self-drive car rental platform.

I have also worked on machine learning projects, including training an XGBoost model on the Titanic dataset, achieving approximately 86% accuracy through data preprocessing, feature engineering, and model optimization. My goal is to become a Machine Learning Engineer and contribute to building impactful AI solutions.
